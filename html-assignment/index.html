About Me

Jenny is a business manager, marketing specialist and student of Interactive Media Design in the Algonquin College, with experience managing teams in different areas and leading projects in digital marketing companies. Some years ago she identified all the administrative needs that small business and entrepreneurs has and decided to focus on help them and make them grow. Now she is being prepared to help those whose want to have and place on internet and provide digital experiences to their customers.

Jenny always tries to learn new things, that made her desire of studying abroad raised, she considered herself a forever student. Jenny loves to travel, she is originally from Colombia, she has visited about 30 cities around the world and she has lived in three countries, EE UU, France and Canada the 3rd . She is fueled by her passion for handcrafted, she has a entrepreneurship about knitting named Handmade Friends, she makes stuff animals in crochet and teach how to make them.
